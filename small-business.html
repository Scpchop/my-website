Small Business Support

Website setup and CRM integration for a local bakery

This project involved supporting a small bakery by creating a website to showcase their products, opening hours, and contact form. I also set up a lightweight customer relationship management (CRM) system to handle online orders and inquiries.

The site was designed to be simple, fast, and mobile-friendly. I used easy-to-manage tools and automated contact responses to streamline their workflow without overwhelming their staff.
