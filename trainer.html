Personal Trainer Website

A one-page fitness coaching site with service listings and a booking form

This website was built for a personal trainer looking to promote their coaching services. It includes a modern hero section, service descriptions, a responsive design, and an integrated contact form for session bookings.

The layout is clean and mobile-friendly, designed for both quick browsing and detailed reading. Tools used include Wix and custom HTML for form integration. All assets were optimized for performance.
